Characters in NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo Ibarra is the main character of Noli Me Tangere and he studied from Europe after being there for 7 years.
Maria Clara She is a side character and a lover to Crisostomo Ibarra and she is a kind woman.
Crispin This person has been child abuse and overworked too much and was framed for taking 32 pesos from someone.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is a cruel friar and is the main villan in the story he is corrupt and very cruel..
Kapitan Tiago He is a very wealthy and he is the one that raised Maria Clara.
